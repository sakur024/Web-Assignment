About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Barishal is dedicated to advancing knowledge and nurturing innovation in the field of computing and technology. Since its inception, the department has become a center of excellence for students passionate about computer science, engineering, and problem-solving. The department offers a dynamic academic environment where talented students and experienced faculty collaborate to explore cutting-edge research, modern computing techniques, and real-world applications. With a curriculum that blends theoretical foundations and practical skills, the CSE Department prepares its graduates to excel in diverse fields such as software development, artificial intelligence, networking, data science, cybersecurity, and research. Our mission is to produce skilled professionals and researchers who will contribute to the technological advancement of Bangladesh and beyond. Through quality education, innovative projects, and participation in national and international competitions, the department continues to inspire the next generation of engineers and leaders.

The department offers Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science and Engineering, and regularly updates the curriculum to maintain international standards. CSE has consistently set benchmarks in research, innovation, and global recognition.
