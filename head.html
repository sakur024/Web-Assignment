Message from the Head of Department

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Barishal. Our department is committed to nurturing bright minds and shaping them into skilled professionals who can lead the future of technology. With a strong foundation in computer science, engineering principles, and ethical values, we strive to prepare our students to excel in academia, industry, and research. At CSE, we believe education goes beyond classrooms. We encourage our students to engage in innovative projects, research activities, and national and international competitions to broaden their knowledge and strengthen their problem-solving abilities. Our dedicated faculty members are passionate about guiding students, fostering creativity, and inspiring innovation. As we continue to grow, our vision remains clear — to produce competent graduates, advance knowledge through research, and contribute to the technological progress of Bangladesh and the global community. We warmly welcome you to explore our department and join us in this exciting journey of learning, discovery, and innovation.

I warmly invite you to explore our programs, research initiatives, and student activities that make CSE at University of Barishal a vibrant place for learning and discovery.
